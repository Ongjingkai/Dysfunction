Alcohol Addiction

Method of quitting

  • Stay hydrated!

    • -in some research, some types of alcohol helps you quench thirst and most people tend to consume alcohol when they are not properly hydrated.

  • Keep attractive non-alcoholic options at home.

    • -keeping your house stocked with good teas, sparkling water, and other non-alcoholic drinks will also help. Substituting alcoholic drinks with tasty non-alcoholic drinks is a good alternative for people choosing to reduce their intake.

  • Set yourself a drink limit and count your drinks.

    • -Keep the max number of drinks to 10/week and 4/drinking session, You’ll find you can do without that extra drink after all and your body will thank you for it the next day.

  • Swap to low or no alcohol alternatives.

    • -Keep the max number of drinks to 10/week and 4/drinking session, You’ll find you can do without that extra drink after all and your body will thank you for it the next day.
